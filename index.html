Currently an Electrical Engineering intern at the Green Bank Observatory. Working on a machine-learning-based, real-time radio frequency interference and FRB/pulsar pulse detection project for radio astronomy observations.

Working towards my BS in Electrical Engineering from the University of Alabama - Tuscaloosa. Minors in Math and the Randall Research Scholars program (formerly known as the Computer Based Honors Program). Interested in machine learning, remote sensing, and autonomous/electric vehicles.

I enjoy solving complex problems at the intersection of hard science, engineering, and software that benefits others. Check out my projects, NaturallyFundamental.com for my interactive science visualizations, or TheClimateGap.com for some of my travel and tech stories.
